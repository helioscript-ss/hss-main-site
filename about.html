About Our Company

Our Mission

Founded in 2019, Helioscript Secure Systems was born from a singular vision: to create a new paradigm of digital security capable of withstanding the threats of tomorrow. Our mission is to provide impenetrable, quantum-resistant encryption and data storage solutions to organizations handling the world's most sensitive information. We believe that true security is proactive, not reactive.

Our Story

The company was founded by a team of veteran cryptographers and cybersecurity experts from the national defense sector. Recognizing the impending threat posed by quantum computing to classical encryption standards, they established Helioscript to pioneer the next generation of cryptographic protocols. Today, we are a trusted partner for leading aerospace and defense contractors worldwide.
